About me

I am Fei Teng, a PhD student at Hunan University, China, supervised by Prof. Kailun Yang. Prior to this, I completed my M.Sc. studies at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, Germany, and received my B.Sc. degree from the China University of Mining and Technology, China.
Research Interests: I aim to make agents less likely to get lost in open worlds and less prone to breaking down when transferred across scenes. I am currently seeking collaborators interested in open-world perception and world modeling. If this resonates with your research interests, feel free to reach out.
